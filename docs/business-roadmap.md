## 🚨 Risks & Mitigation

### Technical Risks
- **Scaling challenges** → Early architecture investment
- **Security breaches** → Continuous security audits
- **Performance issues** → Proactive monitoring

### Business Risks
- **Competition from big tech** → Move fast, focus on developers
- **Market education needed** → Content marketing investment
- **Funding challenges** → Revenue-first approach

### Mitigation Strategies
1. **Diversify revenue** - Not dependent on single source
2. **Build moat** - Network effects and integrations
3. **Stay lean** - Efficient growth metrics
4. **Community first** - Loyal user base
